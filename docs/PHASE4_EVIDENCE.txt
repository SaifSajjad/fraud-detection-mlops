## Phase 4 Recovery — 2026-06-08

### Root Cause

The previous session's `grafana/grafana:latest` image pull was still in progress when the session timed out.
The image completed pulling during the subsequent cluster shutdown, so on restart the image was locally
cached inside the minikube node. Grafana started successfully on its own after `minikube start -p fraud-mlops-p4`.
No manifest change was required.
